Leave Approval System

A simple and effective way to manage, track, and approve employee leave requests within the organization.

Guidelines

  • Submit leave requests with accurate details.
  • You can only have one pending request at a time.
  • Wait for admin approval or rejection.
  • Check your request status regularly.
  • Contact the administrator for any issues.
